Teach. Learn. Earn. Creativity is an infinite loop between teaching and learning. One minute you’re sharing your latest creations with the world, the next you’re delving into the work of the artists you most admire. Nourishing and being nourished. Inspiring and being inspired.

Declaration is a platform built to optimize this loop. We make it easy for creative professionals of every stripe to showcase their work, broadcast their insights, and monetize their intellectual capital — all through a beautiful, intuitive mobile app.

Our goal is to help the entire creative community learn from each other, make new mistakes (instead of repeating the same old ones), and bring about an evolutionary leap in the creative process.
